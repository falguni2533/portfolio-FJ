01 · about

Two disciplines, one habit of finishing things.

I'm currently in my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at GLA University, Mathura, graduating 2028. Most of what I know beyond the classroom came from an internship at Baoiam Innovations, where I sat at the intersection of two roles: Full-Stack Development Intern and UI/UX Lead Intern.

That split shows up in how I build. I wire up REST APIs and component logic in React and Node, but I don't consider a feature done until I've gone back through it in Figma, checked the spacing, and fixed whatever felt clumsy to use.

Outside of coursework, I make videos and design creatives for my college's GeeksforGeeks chapter — which mostly means I'm comfortable with Canva and CapCut too, not just VS Code.
